FMT Cheat Sheet

General
%v (value in default format)
%T (type)
%% (Literal %)

Boolean
%t (true or false)

Integer
%b (base 2) Binary
%o (base 8) Octal
%d (base 10) Decimal
%x (base 16) Hexadecimal

Floating Points
%e (scientific notation)
%f OR %F (decimal no exponent)
%g (for large exponents)

Strings
%s (default)
%q (double quoted string)
